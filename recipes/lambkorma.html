Lamb Korma

Home

Description:

Lamb Korma is a traditional Indian dish known for its rich and creamy sauce made with yogurt, cream, and a blend of aromatic spices. Tender pieces of lamb are slow-cooked to perfection, resulting in a flavorful and comforting meal.

Ingredients:

  • 500g lamb, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps:

  1. In a large pot, heat the ghee or oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they are golden brown.
  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pot and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the lamb pieces to the pot and brown them on all sides.
  4. Stir in the yogurt, heavy cream, garam masala, turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and salt. Mix well to coat the lamb with the spices.
  5. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the lamb is tender and cooked through. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving. Enjoy your Lamb Korma with rice or naan bread!
